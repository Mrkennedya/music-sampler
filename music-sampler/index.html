Kendrick Lamar: The New King of Hip-Hop?

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth, also known as Kung fu Kenny and K-Dot, is an American hip-hop artist born in Compton, California. Winner of 12 Grammy awards and multiple accolades, Lamar's discography spans over 15 years as he has revolutionized the hip-hop industry.
